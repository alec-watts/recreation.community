purpose

Enable happy, healthy experiences through mindful consumption. Everything we consume is a tool to alter our state. Like most tools, it can be used for good or bad but on the whole is a net positive (or it wouldn't still be around).

Build tools that trust and empower individuals to make the right decision. Provide options and information to decide what's best for you at the moment.

DIAL is the subtle, dynamic all-in-one grinder and storage device for all of your needs. Carry whatever you need for the night in the storage container and clip-on magnetic accessories to go.
